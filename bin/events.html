Meetings & Conferences Meetings & Conferences Meetings & Conferences

Organising an event in Perth? Country Comfort® Hotel Inter City Perth is the perfect venue when you want peace and quiet so that your delegates can focus on the business at hand. The function room caters for meetings for up to 60 delegates (theatre style).

Our on-site Function Sales team will be able to assist in making your conference or meeting successful and memorable.

  • 2 purpose built function rooms catering for small meetings of 10 to 60 delegates (theatre style)
  • Function rooms feature natural light
  • Choice of air-conditioning or fresh air
  • Complimentary high speed Wi-Fi included
  • Direct outdoor terrace access
  • Ground floor and wheelchair access
  • Private dining room
  • Business support services
  • Photocopying and courier service
  • AV Equipment available on request

For further information please contact our events team.
