Facilities

Whether you are looking for somewhere to chill, taking a dip in the pool or having a workout, Sage West Perth offers them all within its vicinity.
Lounge

A lounge on steroids, our NEXT Lounge offers boardroom meeting space, a pantry, a library and a fully equipped gym for you to pump those pecs before strolling over to the Pool bar to show them off. And did we mention free tea and coffee?

  • Complimentary tea and coffee machine
  • Light snacks with chargeback to your room payment options
  • Wireless, secure printer
  • Comfy couches to sit and watch TV
  • Boardroom table for meetings
  • Library with books featuring human achievement and design

Spa

The SPA at Kiridara is a boutique spa, tucked amidst dense teak forests. Serene and blissful, the aroma of essential oils and the breezy surrounds of the teak forest truly make the small and petite Kiridara Spa a unique place that demands undivided attention.

  • 3 Treatment rooms, 2 steam rooms
  • Signature Treament: Laotian Herbal Steam
  • Free Pick-up and Drop-off in Luang Prabang
