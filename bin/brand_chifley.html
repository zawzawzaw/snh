Our Locations

Newcastle

Chifley Apartments Newcastle

Chifley Apartments Newcastle provides both short and long-term self-catering style accommodation. Feel at home in one of 51 air-conditioned apartments featuring fully equipped kitchen and laundry facilities, with own private balcony.

Alice Springs

Chifley Alice Springs Resort

Situated on the banks of the famous Todd River, Chifley Alice Springs Resort offers spectacular views of the towering River Red Gum, while the well-manicured lush green lawns of the Resort create a relaxing atmosphere as you soak in the Australian Outback charm of the Northern Territory.

Adelaide

Chifley On South Terrace

The Chifley On South Terrace puts you in the heart of Adelaide, within a 20-minute stroll of popular places like Adelaide Central Market and Victoria Square. The hotel stands across from the South Parklands and makes an ideal base for business and leisure travellers alike.
